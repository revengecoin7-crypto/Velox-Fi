WHAT IS VELOXFI?

VeloxFi is a free-to-play memecoin battle prediction platform built on the Solana blockchain. Players select two of the most volatile memecoins from the top 10 by 24-hour price movement, choose a battle timeframe, and predict which coin will gain the highest percentage within that window.

No wallet required. No entry fee. No financial risk. VeloxFi is designed to be the most accessible on-chain prediction game in crypto — anyone can play, anyone can earn, and the best analysts rise to the top of the global leaderboard.

Correct predictions are rewarded with $BATTLE tokens, the native currency of the VeloxFi ecosystem. $BATTLE tokens will be tradeable on Solana once launched via pump.fun, giving skilled players a real financial reward for their market knowledge.

HOW THE PREDICTION GAME WORKS

Every battle follows a simple, transparent process:

01 — SELECT YOUR COINS
Choose two memecoins from the live top-10 most volatile list, updated in real time using CoinGecko price data with Binance.US fallback to ensure maximum uptime.
02 — PICK A TIMEFRAME
Choose your battle window: 5 minutes, 15 minutes, or 30 minutes. Longer battles carry higher risk but reward more $BATTLE tokens on a correct call.
03 — PREDICT THE WINNER
Pick which coin you believe will gain the highest percentage by the end of the battle window. Entry prices are locked at the moment you confirm your pick.
04 — BATTLE RESOLVES
When the timer expires, VeloxFi fetches the final prices and calculates percentage change from each coin's entry price. The coin with the higher percentage gain wins. Tied battles award no tokens.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Everything you need to know about VeloxFi and the $BATTLE token.
What is VeloxFi?
VeloxFi is a free-to-play memecoin battle prediction platform on Solana. You pick two memecoins, choose a timeframe (5, 15, or 30 minutes), predict which one will gain the most in price percentage, and earn $BATTLE tokens for correct predictions. No wallet, no entry fee, no financial risk — just skill and market knowledge.
How do I earn $BATTLE tokens?
Create a free account, then start a battle. Pick two memecoins and predict the winner. If your pick gains more % by the time the battle ends, you win tokens. Rewards are: 1 token for a 5-minute win, 2 tokens for 15 minutes, 3 tokens for 30 minutes. Tokens are tracked server-side and will be claimable on-chain after the $BATTLE token launches.
Are $BATTLE tokens real crypto?
Not yet — but they will be. $BATTLE tokens are currently tracked in your VeloxFi account as you earn them. Once the token officially launches on pump.fun on Solana, you'll be able to claim your accumulated tokens on-chain and trade them freely. The total supply is fixed at 1 billion $BATTLE with no minting after launch.
Is it free to play?
100% free. You do not need a crypto wallet, Solana SOL, or any payment to play VeloxFi. Just create a free account with a username, email, and password. There are no premium tiers, no paid battles, and no hidden costs. Everyone earns $BATTLE purely through skill.
How are winners determined?
When your battle timer expires, VeloxFi fetches the live price for both coins and calculates the percentage change from each coin's entry price (the price at the moment you confirmed your pick). The coin with the higher percentage gain wins the battle. If both coins move exactly the same %, the battle is a draw and no tokens are awarded.

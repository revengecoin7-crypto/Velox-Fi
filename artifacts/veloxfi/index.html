GAMES GUIDE

VeloxFi Arcade Games: Earn WOLF Playing Retro Crypto Games

April 12, 2026 · 7 min read · By VeloxFi Team

Mining is passive, but VeloxFi's four arcade games let you actively earn WOLF through skill and gameplay. Each game awards up to 120 WOLF per session based on your performance score. This guide explains how each game works, what the scoring looks like, and the tips that separate high earners from casual players.

How Game Rewards Work

Every arcade game on VeloxFi uses a score-to-WOLF conversion system. When your game session ends (by losing or completing), your final score is converted to a WOLF reward scaled up to the 120 WOLF cap. Higher scores earn more WOLF — up to the maximum. If you score high enough to hit the cap, you receive the full 120 WOLF regardless of how much higher your score is.

After your session ends, a CLAIM WOLF button appears. Click it to add the earned WOLF to your balance. This is the same claim flow as mining — it's always manual so you can decide when to claim.

Rocket Miner

Rocket Miner is a space-themed shooter. You pilot a rocket through an asteroid field, dodging incoming rocks and collecting glowing ore deposits worth points. The game gets progressively faster as you survive longer. Your score is the total ore collected before your rocket is destroyed. Focus on ore collection rather than just survival — staying alive but ignoring ore earns fewer WOLF than aggressive collection with calculated risks.

Crypto Snake

Crypto Snake is a crypto-themed take on the classic Snake game. Your snake grows longer each time it collects a WOLF coin on the arena. The longer your snake, the higher your score multiplier — but the harder it is to avoid colliding with your own tail. The optimal strategy is controlled growth: don't rush into cramped areas early when a coin is in the clear. Score accelerates dramatically in the final stretch when the snake is large.

Battle Tetris

Battle Tetris is a crypto-branded Tetris variant. Falling blocks display crypto symbols. Complete full horizontal lines to clear them and score points. Lines cleared in quick succession earn a combo multiplier bonus. The key skill is keeping the board low — don't let pieces stack near the top. Focus on clearing multiple lines at once (a "Tetris" clear of 4 lines at once gives the highest point bonus per block placed).

Wolf Run

Wolf Run is a side-scrolling endless runner set in a neon cyberpunk city. You play as the VeloxFi wolf mascot, jumping and sliding over obstacles while collecting WOLF tokens scattered through the level. The level gets faster over time. Your score is the combination of distance survived plus tokens collected. Prioritize token collection in the early phase when the pace is manageable, then focus on survival as speed increases.

All four games are live now. Play free, earn WOLF, convert to $BATTLE on Solana.

Strategy: Which Game Earns the Most?

All four games share the same 120 WOLF cap, so the most efficient game is the one you can score highest in relative to its cap. Spend a few sessions in each game, note your score patterns, and focus on the one where you most consistently reach the cap. For most players, Snake and Tetris reward pattern recognition (learnable quickly), while Rocket Miner and Wolf Run reward fast reflexes. Play all four to stack up to 480 WOLF per day from games alone.

Top 10 Most Volatile Memecoins Right Now: Live Battle Guide

VeloxFi's memecoin battle prediction feature is built around one core mechanic: a live, auto-updating list of the top 10 most volatile memecoins. This list is the pool you pick from every time you start a battle. Understanding how it works — and what makes a memecoin volatile — gives you a real edge when choosing your predictions.

How VeloxFi Selects the Top 10

Every 5 minutes, VeloxFi automatically pulls live price data from CoinGecko (with a Binance.US fallback for maximum uptime). It ranks all tracked memecoins by their absolute 24-hour percentage price change — the coins that have moved the most in either direction over the past 24 hours appear at the top. The top 10 from that ranking become your battle pool.

This means the list is always fresh. A coin that was on the list this morning may have settled down and dropped off by afternoon, replaced by a newly trending coin with more active momentum. The real-time nature of the list ensures battles are always between genuinely active assets.

What Makes a Memecoin Volatile?

Memecoins are among the most volatile assets in crypto because their prices are driven by narrative and social momentum rather than fundamentals. The key volatility triggers are:

  • Influencer mentions — a single tweet from a major crypto account can send a coin up 200% in hours
  • Exchange listings — a new DEX or CEX listing brings a flood of new buyers
  • Viral memes and trends — a coin tied to a trending cultural moment gains attention fast
  • Community buying events — coordinated group buys on Telegram create sharp spikes
  • Token unlocks and burns — supply changes impact price dramatically on low-liquidity coins

Battle Strategy for the Top 10 List

Since both coins in your battle are already highly volatile, you're not predicting whether they'll move — you're predicting which one will move more. This relative framing is the key insight. Even if both coins drop in value, the one that drops less is your winner.

  • Look at recent momentum — a coin already trending upward in the last hour is more likely to continue in a short battle window
  • Check trading volume — volume spikes often precede price spikes; a coin with rising volume is building momentum
  • Compare social activity — search both coins on Telegram and Twitter right before your battle to see which has fresher buzz
  • Use longer timeframes for fundamentals — the 30-minute battle window gives more time for underlying momentum to play out vs. pure noise
